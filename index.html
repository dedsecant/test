Mangalore Refinery and Petrochemicals Limited (MRPL) is a prominent name in the energy and petrochemical industry. Based in Mangalore, India, MRPL plays a significant role in refining crude oil to produce a range of high-quality petroleum products and petrochemicals. With a legacy marked by dedication and innovation, MRPL has grown into a key player, contributing to the energy needs of modern life.

Committed to excellence and sustainability, MRPL is known for its diverse product portfolio, including fuels that power vehicles, raw materials for plastics, and chemicals utilized across various industries. As a responsible corporate entity, MRPL strives to balance industrial growth with environmental stewardship and community engagement.

With a focus on quality, innovation, and a forward-looking approach, MRPL continues to shape the energy landscape, ensuring a reliable supply of energy products while actively contributing to the nation's progress.
